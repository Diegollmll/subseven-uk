❌ **Papers get lost.**
❌ **Nobody likes filling them out.**
❌ **You don't know who actually did the checks.**

✅ **ForkU fixes this** by giving every operator a **simple, one-tap inspection checklist** on their phone.

No messy forms. No excuses. And best of all? **You have a digital record of every inspection in case OSHA comes knocking.**
